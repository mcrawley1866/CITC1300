Paradise

Paradise is a new TV show that came out in January of 2025. The show is categorized as a political thriller that is starring Sterling K. Brown and follows Secret Service agent Xavier Collins as he investigates the assassination of President Cal Bradford in a secret underground bunker following a global catastrophe.

Paradise

"Paradise" is a compelling new TV series that follows the lives of characters in a seemingly idyllic small town, where the surroundings hide multiple complex relationships and secrets. The story continues on to explore new desires, disloyalty, and even redemption as characters confront their pasts and navigate intertwined lives. As they face personal dilemmas and societal pressures, the show reveals the darker undercurrents beneath the facade of paradise, illustrating that not everything is as perfect as it seems.
